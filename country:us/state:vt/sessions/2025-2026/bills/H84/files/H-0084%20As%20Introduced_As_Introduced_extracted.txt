BILL AS INTRODUCED H.84
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
2 Sec. 1. 18 V.S.A. § 9361 is amended to read:
3 § 9361. HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS DELIVERING HEALTH CARE
4 SERVICES THROUGH TELEMEDICINE OR BY STORE-AND-
5 FORWARD MEANS
6 * * *
7 (d) Neither a health care provider nor a patient shall create or cause to be
8 created a recording of a provider’s telemedicine consultation with a patient
9 unless both the patient and the provider consent to the recording.
10 * * *
11 Sec. 2. 18 V.S.A. § 9362 is amended to read:
12 § 9362. HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS DELIVERING HEALTH CARE
13 SERVICES BY AUDIO-ONLY TELEPHONE
14 * * *
15 (d) Neither a health care provider nor a patient shall create or cause to be
16 created a recording of a provider’s telephone consultation with a patient unless
17 both the patient and the provider consent to the recording.
18 * * *
19 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
20 This act shall take effect on passage.
