1 H.324
2 Introduced by Representative Burditt of West Rutland
3 Referred to Committee on
4 Date:
5 Subject: Executive Branch; retirement; State Employees’ Retirement System
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to raise the
7 mandatory retirement age for Group C members of the Vermont State
8 Retirement System.
9 An act relating to mandatory retirement age of the Vermont State
10 Retirement System
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 3 V.S.A. § 459 is amended to read:
13 § 459. NORMAL AND EARLY RETIREMENT
14 (a) Normal retirement.
15 * * *
16 (2) Group C members. Any Group C member who is an officer or
17 employee of the Department of Public Safety assigned to police and law
18 enforcement duties, including the Commissioner of Public Safety appointed
19 before July 1, 2000, and who has reached his or her the member’s normal
20 retirement date may retire on a normal retirement allowance, on the first day of
VT LEG #380679 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.324
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 any month after the member may have separated from service, by filing an
2 application in the manner outlined in subdivision (3) of this subsection. Any
3 Group C member in service shall be retired on a normal retirement allowance
4 on the first day of the calendar month next following attainment of 57 65 years
5 of age. Notwithstanding, it is provided that any such member who is an
6 official appointed for a term of years may remain in service until the end of the
7 member’s term of office or any extension thereto, resulting from
8 reappointment.
9 * * *
10 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
11 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
VT LEG #380679 v.1
