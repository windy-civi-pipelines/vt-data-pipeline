2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representatives Bluemle of Burlington, Stone of Burlington,
4 Garofano of Essex, Graning of Jericho, and Mrowicki of Putney
5 Referred to Committee on
6 Date:
7 Subject: Human services; Department for Children and Families; residential
8 programs; seclusion and restraint
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to reduce the use
10 of seclusion and restraint on children and youth who are in the custody of the
11 Department for Children and Families and participating in a residential
12 program. This bill proposes to improve seclusion and restraint data collection
13 by defining seclusion and restraint in 33 V.S.A. § 3206 and requiring more
14 consistent reporting by residential programs serving children and youth in the
15 custody of the Department. It proposes to require that ongoing modifications
16 to the Department’s IT system include processes for collecting and integrating
17 data pertaining to seclusion and restraint. Lastly, this bill proposes to require
18 enhanced professional development on alternatives to seclusion and restraint
19 for the staff of residential programs serving children and youth in the custody
20 of the Department.
VT LEG #379370 v.1
