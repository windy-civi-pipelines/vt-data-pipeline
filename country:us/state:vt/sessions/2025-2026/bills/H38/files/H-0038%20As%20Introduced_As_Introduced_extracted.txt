8 Referred to Committee on
9 Date:
10 Subject: Commerce and trade; Human Rights Commission
11 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize and
12 provide funding for six additional full-time positions and two additional part-
13 time positions within the Human Rights Commission.
14 An act relating to adding new positions to the Human Rights Commission
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 Sec. 1. HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION; POSITIONS; APPROPRIATION
17 (a) The following new positions are created in the Human Rights
18 Commission:
19 (1) one full-time, permanent, classified Communications and Outreach
20 Coordinator;
VT LEG #379115 v.2
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.38
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 (2) one full-time, permanent, classified Intake Specialist;
2 (3) three full-time, permanent, exempt Staff Attorney Investigators;
3 (4) one full-time, permanent, exempt Paralegal;
4 (5) one part-time, permanent, classified Mediator; and
5 (6) one part-time, permanent, classified Social Worker/Case Manager.
6 (b) In fiscal year 2026, the following amounts are appropriated from the
7 General Fund to the Human Rights Commission:
8 (1) $25,000.00 for education and outreach activities; and
9 (2) for the positions created in subsection (a) of this section:
10 (A) $794,616.00 for salary and benefits;
11 (B) $18,000.00 for related operating expenses and equipment; and
12 (C) $50,000.00 for additional office space.
13 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
14 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
