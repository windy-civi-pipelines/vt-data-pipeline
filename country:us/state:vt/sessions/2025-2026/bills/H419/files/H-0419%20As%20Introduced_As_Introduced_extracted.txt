1 H.419
2 Introduced by Representatives Boutin of Barre City and Galfetti of Barre
3 Town
4 Referred to Committee on
5 Date:
6 Subject: Taxation and finance; education property tax; tax increment financing
7 districts; municipal and county government; indebtedness; statewide
8 tax increment financing; TIF
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to extend by two
10 years the period during which the City of Barre can incur indebtedness for its
11 tax increment financing district.
12 An act relating to the City of Barre tax increment financing district
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 2023 Acts and Resolves No. 72, Sec. 37 is amended to read:
15 Sec. 37. TAX INCREMENT FINANCING DISTRICT; CITY OF BARRE;
16 EXTENSION; INCREMENT
17 (a) Notwithstanding 2021 Acts and Resolves No. 73, Sec. 26a, amending
18 2020 Acts and Resolves No. 175, Sec. 29, or any other provision of law, the
19 authority of the City of Barre to incur indebtedness is hereby extended to
20 March 31, 2026 2028.
VT LEG #381082 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.419
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 (b) Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the authority of the City of
2 Barre to retain municipal and education tax increment is hereby extended until
3 June 30, 2039.
4 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
5 This act shall take effect on passage.
