EssexJunction, Garofano of Essex, Houghton of EssexJunction, and Steady of

Hornets girls’ gymnastics team on winning a second consecutive State
championship

team members competing in the current season, the Hornets savored the
potential of a repeat performance, and
Whereas, an unusual complication on the day of the State championship
meet, held at Champlain Valley Union High School, occurred when Head
Coach Mary Savage was absent giving birth to her first child, a baby boy,
requiring Assistant Coach Anna Charland to assume responsibility for
supporting the Essexgymnasts, and

the top team score in the bars, floor, and beam events, enabling them to clinch
the 2025 crown by defeating second-place St. Johnsbury Academy 141.850 to

Raw Text:
State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 75
Offered by Representatives Dodge of Essex, Black of Essex, Dolan of
EssexJunction, Garofano of Essex, Houghton of EssexJunction, and Steady of
Milton
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2025 EssexHigh School
Hornets girls’ gymnastics team on winning a second consecutive State
championship
Whereas, with fresh memories of their 2024 State title and many returning
team members competing in the current season, the Hornets savored the
potential of a repeat performance, and
Whereas, an unusual complication on the day of the State championship
meet, held at Champlain Valley Union High School, occurred when Head
Coach Mary Savage was absent giving birth to her first child, a baby boy,
requiring Assistant Coach Anna Charland to assume responsibility for
supporting the Essexgymnasts, and
Whereas, the Hornets strove to honor their coach with a victory and earned
the top team score in the bars, floor, and beam events, enabling them to clinch
the 2025 crown by defeating second-place St. Johnsbury Academy 141.850 to
135.800, and
Whereas, talented athletes Jocelyn Bourget, Sarai Crowley, Jill Diehl, Leah
Frisbie, Riley Hammond, Anna Kinney, Maybel Lyon-Home, Emerson
Marckres, and Rachel Mitchell were delighted to honor their coach on this
special day for both the team and her, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2025 EssexHigh School
Hornets girls’ gymnastics team on winning a second consecutive State
championship, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this
resolution to EssexHigh School.
