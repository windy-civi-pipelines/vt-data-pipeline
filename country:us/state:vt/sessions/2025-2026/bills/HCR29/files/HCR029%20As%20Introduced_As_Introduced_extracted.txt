State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 29
Offered by All Members of the House
Offered by Senators Brock and Norris
her outstanding athletic achievement at the 2024 Paris Olympics
Whereas, Elle Purrier St. Pierre has experienced great success in national and international
running competitions, admirably exhibiting a dual dedication to her sport and to the cherished
family farm life she joyfully shares with her husband, Jamie Purrier, and their young son,
Ivan, and
Whereas, Vermonters first took notice of this gifted runner when she was a member of
Richford High School’s 2013 Division IV girls track and field team, and at the University of
New Hampshire, Elle St. Pierre earned all-American running honors on 11 occasions and
clocked the second fastest ever NCAA woman’s time for the indoor one mile, and
Whereas, in 2020, at the New York Runners Wanamaker Mile, she set the American record
for this same event; in 2021, at the New Balance Indoor Grand Prix, Elle Purrier St. Pierre set
the women’s American indoor two mile record; and in 2022, she won the silver medal in the
women’s 3000m (Indoor) at the world championships, and
Whereas, Elle Purrier St. Pierre has twice achieved the greatest athletic honor of being named
to the 2020 and 2024 U.S. Olympic teams, and
Whereas, running in the women’s 1500m, she earned tenth place in the final round in Tokyo
in 2021 (the Olympics having been delayed from 2020), and in Paris in 2024, Elle Purrier St.
Pierre secured an improved eighth-place final round finish, and she can be extremely proud of
these personal running milestones, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates Montgomery runner Elinor Purrier St. Pierre on her
outstanding athletic achievement at the 2024 Paris Olympics, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Elle
Purrier St. Pierre.

House concurrent resolution congratulating Montgomery runner Elinor Purrier St. Pierre on

Whereas, Elle Purrier St. Pierre has experienced great success in national and international
