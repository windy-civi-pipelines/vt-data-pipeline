5 Subject: Fish and wildlife; technical corrections; definitions; uniform points;
6 transporting a crossbow in a motor vehicle
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to make several
8 technical corrections to fish and wildlife statutes. The bill would strike two
9 definitions from the general definitions section of fish and wildlife statutes.
10 The bill also would make technical changes to the uniform point system
11 statute, including increasing the point penalty for snaring animals. The bill
12 would also provide that certain use of public waters violations would be
13 judicial bureau offenses.
14 An act relating to technical corrections to fish and wildlife statutes
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
