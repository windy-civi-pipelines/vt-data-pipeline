6 Subject: Commerce and trade; consumer protection; digital advertiser brokers
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to restrict certain
8 digital advertisers from being able to own both a buying and selling digital
9 advertisement brokerage and to require certain digital advertisers to adhere to
10 ethical business practices, including the use of reasonable diligence in avoiding
