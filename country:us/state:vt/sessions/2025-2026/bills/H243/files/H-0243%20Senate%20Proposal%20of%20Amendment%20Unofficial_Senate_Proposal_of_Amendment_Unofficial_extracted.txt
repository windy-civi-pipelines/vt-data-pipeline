7 § 1656. SERVICE OF PROCESS; SECRETARY OF STATE AS AGENT

9 Sec. 4a. 11 V.S.A. chapter 15, subchapter 2 is amended to read:
10 Subchapter 2. Administrative Authority
11 * * *
12 § 1657. CERTIFICATE OF GOOD STANDING
13 Upon request of any person and payment of the applicable fee, the Secretary
14 of State shall issue a certificate of good standing for a business organization
15 that is authorized to do business in this State, and that is currently active and in
16 good standing as of the date the certificate is issued, as reflected in the records
17 of the Secretary.
18 Third: By adding a new section to be Sec. 7a to read as follows:

1 (a) A person may request the Secretary of State to furnish a certificate of
2 existence for a limited liability company or a certificate of authorization for a
3 foreign limited liability company.
4 (b) A certificate of existence for a limited liability company shall set forth:
5 (1) the company’s name;
6 (2) that it is duly organized under the laws of this State and the date of
7 organization; and
8 (3) that articles of termination have not been filed.
9 (c) A certificate of authorization for a foreign limited liability company
10 shall set forth:
11 (1) the company’s name used in this State;
12 (2) that it is authorized to transact business in this State; and
13 (3) that a certificate of cancellation has not been filed.
14 (d) Subject to any qualification stated in the certificate, a certificate of
15 existence or authorization issued by the Secretary of State may be relied upon
16 as conclusive evidence that the domestic or foreign limited liability company is
17 in existence or is authorized to transact business in this State A person may
18 request the Secretary of State to issue a certificate of good standing for a
19 domestic or foreign limited liability company pursuant to section 1657 of this
20 title.
