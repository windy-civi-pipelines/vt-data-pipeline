5 Subject: Education; Vermont State Colleges Corporation; University of
6 Vermont; board of trustees; shared governance
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to (1) eliminate
8 two legislative trustees from the Board of Trustees of the Vermont State
9 Colleges Corporation and add one faculty trustee and one staff trustee, to serve
10 four-year terms; and (2) add one faculty and one staff trustee to the Board of
11 Trustees of the University of Vermont and State Agricultural College, to serve
12 four-year terms.
13 An act relating to the governance of the Vermont State Colleges
14 Corporation and the University of Vermont
15 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
16 * * * Vermont State Colleges Corporation Governance * * *
17 Sec. 1. 16 V.S.A. § 2172 is amended to read:
18 § 2172. TRUSTEES; APPOINTMENT; VACANCIES
19 (a) The Corporation shall be governed by a board of 15 17 trustees who
20 shall be appointed or elected as follows:

1 (1) Biennially, the Governor, with the advice and consent of the Senate,
2 shall appoint trustees to serve for four-year terms expiring March 1 of the year
3 of the biennial session. Five trustees may be in office at one time under this
4 subdivision. In the event of any vacancy occurring between biennial sessions
5 in an office under this subdivision, the Governor, pursuant to 3 V.S.A. § 257,
6 shall fill the vacancy, and the term of a person so appointed shall expire on
7 March 1 in the year of the next following biennial session.
8 (2)(A) One trustee shall be a student trustee:
9 (i) who is a matriculated student at an educational institution
10 operated by the Vermont State Colleges Corporation;
11 (ii) who is pursuing a degree program; and
12 (iii) who has reached the age of majority.
13 (B) The student trustee shall serve a one-year term expiring on June
14 1. The student trustee shall be appointed, and a vacancy may be filled, from
15 among those eligible students applying for the position by the decision of those
16 members of the steering committee of the Vermont State Colleges Student
17 Association who have been elected at large to that committee by the students at
18 their respective colleges. No student trustee may serve more than two
19 consecutive terms.
20 (3) Four trustees shall be legislative trustees who are members of the
21 General Assembly at the time of their election. Legislative trustees shall serve

1 four-year terms expiring on March 1 of the second year of the biennial session,
2 and they shall be elected by joint assembly of the Legislature. Vacancies for
3 any cause shall be filled by the General Assembly at its earliest opportunity
4 and the term of a person so appointed shall expire on March 1 of the next even
5 numbered year.
6 (4) Four trustees shall be elected by the Board of Trustees to four-year
7 terms expiring on March 1. Vacancies for any cause shall be filled by the
8 remaining members of the Board of Trustees, and the term of the person so
9 appointed shall expire on the next following March 1.
10 (5) One trustee shall be a faculty member employed by the Vermont
11 State Colleges Corporation and appointed by the executive committee of the
12 union or unions representing faculty to a four-year term expiring on August 1.
13 Vacancies for any cause shall be filled through an appointment by the
14 executive committee of the union or unions representing faculty, and the term
15 of the person so appointed shall expire on the next following August 1.
16 (6) One trustee shall be a staff member employed by the Vermont State
17 Colleges Corporation. The staff trustee shall be appointed by the union or
18 unions representing staff. Vacancies for any cause shall be filled through an
19 appointment by the union or unions representing staff and the term of the
20 person so appointed shall expire on the next following August 1.
