3 Introduced by Representatives Lalley of Shelburne, Austin of Colchester,
4 Cooper of Pownal, Dodge of Essex, Headrick of Burlington,
5 Keyser of Rutland City, Logan of Burlington, Nugent of South
6 Burlington, Pezzo of Colchester, Rachelson of Burlington,
7 Tomlinson of Winooski, and Torre of Moretown
8 Referred to Committee on
9 Date:
10 Subject: Human services; continuing care retirement communities; assessment
11 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
12 Agency of Human Services to conduct an assessment regarding the benefits of
13 increasing the number of continuing care retirement communities in the State’s
14 designated growth centers. The assessment shall examine the efficient
15 provision of health care to elders and their communities; expanding housing
16 and other independent living options; social opportunities for elders; walkable,
17 bikeable, and transit-friendly communities; and accessible, inclusive public
18 spaces that encourage smart growth and infill development.
19 An act relating to continuing care retirement communities
