Section 1:
State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 143
Offered by Senators Collamore, Weeks, and Williams
House concurrent resolution honoring former Pawlet First Constable David
Ricard Sr. for his outstanding municipal public service
Whereas, the municipal government employment of Dave Ricard for the
Town of Pawlet started in 2003 when he was first elected as the town’s first
constable, and
Whereas, over the course of 22 years, he improved the professionalism of
policing services in the Town of Pawlet, assisting local colleagues who
followed his path and earned their law enforcement officer certifications, and
Whereas, although Dave Ricard’s primary public service responsibility was
working as Pawlet First Constable, which he executed professionally and
proficiently, he also performed other municipal roles with the same degree of
attention and excellence, including serving as the West Pawlet Volunteer Fire
Chief; as a patrol officer in Fair Haven; and as the Wells highway foreman,
keeping tabs on that community’s town road conditions, and
Whereas, after over two decades of overseeing law enforcement activities in
the Town of Pawlet, in 2025, Dave Ricard declined to seek reelection,
concluding his exemplary career as the town’s first constable, and his fellow
citizens greatly appreciate his contribution to civic life in their community,
now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly honors former Pawlet First Constable David
Ricard Sr. for his outstanding municipal public service, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this
resolution to Dave Ricard.
