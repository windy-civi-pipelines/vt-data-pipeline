State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 5
Gregoire of Fairfield, and Walker of Swanton
Offered by Senators Brock and Norris
House concurrent resolution congratulating the 2024 Richford Junior-Senior High School
Rockets Division IV championship softball team
Whereas, the strength of the fourth-ranked Richford Rockets initial seventh-inning lead
proved crucial in their 2024 Division IV championship victory in Castleton against the second-
seeded West Rutland Golden Horde, and
Whereas, Richford exhibited impressive offensive prowess early in the contest, scoring six
runs in the second inning, expanding its lead in the third to 8–0, and to 9–0 in the fourth, prior to
West Rutland’s crossing the plate for a single run in the fifth and two in the sixth, and
Whereas, in the decisive seventh inning, the Rockets increased their scoring advantage to 11–
3 in the opening frame, and Richford stoically withstood a four-run Golden Horde final burst of
energy to clinch the 2024 crown 11–7, and
Whereas, the delighted Rockets roster consisted of Jazzy Cunningham, Kyrielle Deuso,
Agnus Heinrich, Vanessa Tessier, Adi Chagnon, Katelyn Doe, Erin Raymond, Sierra Derby,
Maddie Combs, Kayliese McCray, Sophia Derby, Maddie Smith, and Mikayla Chagnon, and
Whereas, Head Coach Dennis Blaney and assistant coaches Randi Chagnon and Kiley Deuso
motivated the Rockets to excel on the softball diamond, now therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates the 2024 Richford Junior-Senior High School
Rockets Division IV championship softball team, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to
Richford Junior-Senior High School.
