1 H.41
3 Sec. 1. 13 V.S.A. § 3761a is added to read:
4 § 3761a. ABUSE OF THE DEAD BODY OF A PERSON
5 (a) No person shall, knowingly without legal authorization, intentionally
6 burn, mutilate, disfigure, dismember, or destroy the dead body of a person.
7 (b)(1) No person shall intentionally bury, transport, hide, conceal, burn,
8 mutilate, disfigure, dismember, or destroy the dead body of a person for the
9 purpose of concealing a crime or avoiding apprehension, prosecution, or
10 conviction of a crime.
11 (2) No person shall commit sexual conduct upon the dead body of a
12 person.
13 (c)(1) A person who violates subsection (a) of this section shall be
14 imprisoned not more than five years or fined not more than $5,000.00, or both.
15 (2) A person who violates subsection (b) of this section shall be
16 imprisoned not more than 15 years or fined not more than $10,000.00, or both.
17 (d) As used in this section:
18 (1) “Dead body of a person” does not include the cremated remains of a
19 person.
20 (2) “Sexual conduct” means any of the following committed against the
21 dead body of a person:

1 (A) any conduct involving contact between the penis and the vulva,
2 the penis and the penis, the penis and the anus, the mouth and the penis, the
3 mouth and the anus, the vulva and the vulva, or the mouth and the vulva;
4 (B) any intrusion, however slight, by any part of an individual’s body
5 or any object into any part of a dead human body with the intent of arousing,
6 appealing to, or gratifying the lust, passions, or sexual desire of any individual;
7 (C) any touching of the dead human body with the intent of arousing,
8 appealing to, or gratifying the lust, passions, or sexual desire of any individual;
9 (D) masturbation; or
10 (E) bestiality.
11 Sec. 2. 18 V.S.A. § 5211 is amended to read:
12 § 5211. UNAUTHORIZED BURIAL OR REMOVAL; PENALTY
13 A person who buries, entombs, transports, or removes the dead body of a
14 person without a burial-transit permit so to do, or in any other manner or at any
15 other time or place than as specified in such permit, shall be imprisoned not
16 more than five years or fined not more than $1,000.00, or both.
17 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
18 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
