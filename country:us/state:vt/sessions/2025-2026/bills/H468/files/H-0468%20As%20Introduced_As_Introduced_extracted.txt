7 Subject: Internal security and public safety; emergency management;
8 miscellaneous amendments relating to flood response and resilience
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to establish State
10 programs that offer technical assistance to municipalities for planning flood-
11 resilient infrastructure, including roadwork adaptations, and applying for
12 federal and State infrastructure grants. This bill also proposes to support
13 specialized flood mitigation projects, including lifting or removing flood-prone
14 bridges and removing dams that pose significant flood risks to nearby
15 communities.
