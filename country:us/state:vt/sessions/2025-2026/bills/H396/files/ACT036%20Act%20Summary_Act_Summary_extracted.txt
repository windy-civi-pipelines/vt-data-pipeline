Act No. 36 (H.396). An act relating to the creation of the Mollie Beattie Distinguished
Service Award
Subjects: Conservation and development; Department of Forests, Parks and
Recreation; Mollie Beattie Distinguished Service Award
This act creates the Mollie Beattie Distinguished Service Award that the
Commissioner of Forests, Parks and Recreation will present annually to either a current
or former State employee or partner whose contributions honor the legacy of Mollie
Beattie in advancing the conservation, accessibility, quality of recreational experience, or
sustainability of Vermont’s public lands.
Effective Date: July 1, 2025
VT LEG #383724 v.2
