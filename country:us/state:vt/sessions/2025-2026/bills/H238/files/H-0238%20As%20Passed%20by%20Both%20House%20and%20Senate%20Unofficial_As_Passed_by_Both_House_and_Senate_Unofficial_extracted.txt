18 ascertainable by the manufacturer.

4 Sec. 12. ANR REPORT ON AVAILABILITY OF PFAS-FREE PERSONAL
5 PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT
6 (a) As used in this section:
7 (1) “Perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances” or “PFAS” means a
8 class of fluorinated organic chemicals containing at least one fully fluorinated
9 carbon atom.
10 (2) “Personal protective equipment” means clothing designed, intended,
11 or marketed to be worn by firefighting personnel in the performance of their
12 duties, designed with the intent for use in fire and rescue activities, and
13 includes jackets, pants, shoes, gloves, helmets, and respiratory equipment.
14 (b) On or before December 15, 2028, the Agency of Natural Resources,
15 after consultation with the Department of Public Safety, shall report to the
16 Senate Committees on Health and Welfare and on Natural Resources and
17 Energy and the House Committees on Human Service and on Environment
18 regarding the availability of personal protective equipment that does not
19 include PFAS. The report shall include:
20 (1) a summary of the general availability in the State of personal
21 protective equipment that does not include PFAS, including whether
