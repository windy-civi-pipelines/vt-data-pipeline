1 H.473
2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representative Austin of Colchester
4 Referred to Committee on
5 Date:
6 Subject: Fish and wildlife; wildlife reintroduction; catamount
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
8 Department of Fish and Wildlife to conduct a feasibility study of the
9 reintroduction of the puma concolor, commonly referred to as the cougar or
10 catamount, into the State of Vermont. The feasibility study would address how
11 the catamount would be reintroduced, the locations in the State suitable for
12 reintroduction, how to manage conflicts between humans and reintroduced
13 catamount, a metric for determining success of reintroduction, an estimated
14 timeline for reintroduction, and an estimated cost of reintroduction. The
15 Commissioner of Fish and Wildlife would be required to submit the results of
16 the feasibility study to the House Committee on Environment and the Senate
17 Committee on Natural Resources and Energy on or before January 1, 2027.
18 An act relating to the reintroduction of catamounts to the State of Vermont
19 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
