1 H.269
2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representatives Noyes of Wolcott, Birong of Vergennes,
4 Goldman of Rockingham, and McGill of Bridport
5 Referred to Committee on
6 Date:
7 Subject: Human services; executive; Treasurer
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to direct the
9 federal disability and survivor benefits of youth in State custody (foster
10 children and justice-involved youth) to an account at the Office of the
11 Treasurer, with a study committee to recommend how to distribute the funds
12 back to the individual when the individual ages out of State custody. This bill
13 also proposes to task the Department for Children and Families with leveraging
14 federal funds available for legal representation in foster care proceedings
15 pursuant to 45 C.F.R. Part 1356 to offset any resulting reduction in funding for
16 the Department.
17 An act relating to the federal benefits of youth in State custody
18 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
