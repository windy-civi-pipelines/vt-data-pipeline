1 H.464
2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representatives Waszazak of Barre City and Galfetti of Barre
4 Town
5 Referred to Committee on
6 Date:
7 Subject: Internal security and public safety; emergency management;
8 miscellaneous amendments relating to flood response and resilience
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
10 Division of Emergency Management to establish the Statewide Framework for
11 Flood Preparedness, Response, and Recovery; provide assistance to
12 municipalities to develop various plans for flood response and recovery; and
13 collaborate with other State agencies to perform a comprehensive needs
14 assessment concerning flood recovery programs and report to the General
15 Assembly on or before December 15, 2025 with the results of the assessment.
16 This bill also proposes to require the Division of Emergency Management to
17 offer financial and technical assistance to municipalities for emergency parking
18 plans, designated emergency shelters, vulnerable resident check-in systems,
19 real-time emergency notification systems, and training for post-disaster roles
20 such as Individual Assistance Coordinators.
VT LEG #381202 v.1
