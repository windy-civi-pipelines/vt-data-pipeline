4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. CHARTER AMENDMENT APPROVAL
6 The General Assembly approves the amendments to the charter of the City
7 of Rutland as set forth in this act. Voters approved the proposals of
8 amendment on November 5, 2024 and March 4, 2025.
9 Sec. 2. 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 9 is amended to read:
10 CHAPTER 9. CITY OF RUTLAND
11 * * *
12 § 5.3. VIOLATION OF ORDINANCES
13 (a) The Board of Aldermen may provide penalties for the violation of any
14 ordinance, regulation, or bylaw. In no case shall the penalty exceed
15 imprisonment for one year or a fine of $500.00, or both, per violation the
16 maximum penalty allowed under 24 V.S.A. § 1974.
17 * * *
18 § 8.5. ASSESSMENT OF TAXES
19 The Board of Aldermen shall annually, during the first twenty (20) 20 days
20 of July, assess upon the grand list of the City taxes sufficient to meet the
21 following requirement:

4 § 17.3. REQUISITIONS
5 (a) All purchases made under section 17.1 of this charter shall be made
6 upon written requisitions therefor upon the Purchasing Agent. The Purchasing
7 Agent shall immediately proceed to purchase under the provisions of the
8 ordinances the property so required and to distribute it accordingly taking a
9 receipt therefor upon the original requisition. The Purchasing Agent shall then
10 forward the requisition to the City Clerk. Purchases in excess of $3,000.00 the
11 amount identified in the City’s Purchasing Policy shall be awarded upon bids
12 as hereinafter set forth in this subchapter.
13 * * *
14 § 36.5. BOARD OF COMMISSIONERS,; COMPOSITION
15 (a) The administration, management, and supervision of the Authority shall
16 be vested in a Board of Commissioners, except as provided otherwise herein.
17 The Board shall consist of nine members, all of whom shall be residents of the
18 City of Rutland for their respective terms of office. One member of the Board
19 shall be the City Treasurer, and one member will be appointed from the Board
20 of Aldermen for a one year term. The remaining seven members of the Board
21 shall be appointed by the Mayor with the approval of the Board of Aldermen

1 pursuant to the charter of the City of Rutland. All members appointed except
2 for members appointed for creation of this Board shall serve for periods of
3 three years. Upon creation, three members shall be appointed for a term to
4 expire on April 1 of the third calendar year after appointment; two members
5 shall be appointed for a term to expire April 1 of the second calendar year after
6 appointment; and two members shall be appointed for a term to expire on April
7 1 of the first calendar year after appointment. All terms shall thereafter expire
8 on April 1 of the third calendar year following appointment. A person

15 term;
16 (3) six members, who shall be residents of the City for their respective
17 terms of office, appointed by the Mayor with the approval of the Board of
18 Aldermen pursuant to this charter; and
19 (4) one member, who shall be a property owner within the City and a
20 resident of Rutland County, appointed by the Mayor with the approval of the
21 Board of Aldermen pursuant to this charter.

1 (b) Except for members appointed for the creation of the Board, members
2 shall serve for three-year terms.
3 (c) Upon creation, three members shall be appointed for a term to expire on
4 April 1 of the third year after appointment, two members shall be appointed for

19 entered upon the minutes of the Authority, and that person will shall refrain
20 from any participation in that matter, but will shall not be precluded from
21 involvement in any other matters.
