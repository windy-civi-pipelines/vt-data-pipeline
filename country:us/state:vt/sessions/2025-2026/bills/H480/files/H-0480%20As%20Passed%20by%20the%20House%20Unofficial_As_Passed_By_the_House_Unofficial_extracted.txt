7 * * * Virtual Learning * * *
8 Sec. 4. 16 V.S.A. § 948 is added to read:
9 § 948. VIRTUAL LEARNING
10 (a) The Agency of Education shall maintain access to and oversight of a
11 virtual learning provider for the purpose of offering virtual learning
12 opportunities to Vermont students.
13 (b) A student may enroll in virtual learning if:
14 (1) the student is enrolled in a Vermont public school, including a
15 Vermont career technical center;
16 (2) virtual learning is determined to be an appropriate learning pathway
17 outlined in the student’s personalized learning plan; and
18 (3) the student’s learning experience occurs under the supervision of an
19 appropriately licensed educator and aligns with State expectations and
20 standards, as adopted by the Agency and the State Board of Education, as
21 applicable.

1 (c) A school district shall count a student enrolled in virtual learning in the
2 school district’s average daily membership, as defined in section 4001 of this
3 title, if the student meets all of the criteria in subsection (b) of this section.
4 Sec. 5. 16 V.S.A. § 942(13) is amended to read:
5 (13) “Virtual learning” means learning in which the teacher and student
6 communicate concurrently through real-time telecommunication. “Virtual
7 learning” also means online learning in which communication between the
8 teacher and student does not occur concurrently and the student works
9 according to his or her own schedule an intentionally designed learning
10 environment for online teaching and learning using online design principles
11 and teachers trained in the delivery of online instruction. This instruction may
12 take place either in a self-paced environment or a real-time environment.
