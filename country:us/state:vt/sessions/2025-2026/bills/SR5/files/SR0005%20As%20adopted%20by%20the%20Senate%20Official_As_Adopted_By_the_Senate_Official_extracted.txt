State of Vermont
Senate Chamber
Montpelier, Vermont
Senate Resolution
Offered by Senator Ram Hinsdale,
organization of the Senate.
Resolved by the Senate:
That the Secretary be directed to inform the House of Representatives
that a quorum of the Senate has assembled and organized by the election of
John H. Bloomer, Jr., of Wallingford, as Secretary, and Philip E. Baruth, a
Senator from Chittenden-Central District, as President pro tempore, and is
ready on its part to proceed with the business of the session.
