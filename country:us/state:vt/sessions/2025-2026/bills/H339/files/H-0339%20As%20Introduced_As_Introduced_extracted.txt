1 H.339
2 Introduced by the Committee on Government Operations and Military Affairs
3 Date:
4 Subject: Alcoholic beverages; licensing; retail licenses; sale of alcoholic
5 beverages for off-premises consumption; repeal
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to remove the
7 repeal of 7 V.S.A. § 230 (sale of alcoholic beverages for off-premises
8 consumption).
9 An act relating to removing the repeal of 7 V.S.A. § 230
10 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
11 Sec. 1. 2021 Acts and Resolves No. 70, Sec. 7, as amended by 2023 Acts and
12 Resolves No. 67, Sec. 8, is further amended to read:
13 Sec. 7. REPEAL
14 7 V.S.A. § 230 is repealed on July 1, 2025. [Repealed.]
15 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
16 This act shall take effect on passage.
