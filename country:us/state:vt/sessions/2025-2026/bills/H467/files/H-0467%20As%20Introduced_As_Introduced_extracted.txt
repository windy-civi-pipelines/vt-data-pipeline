3 Introduced by Representatives Waszazak of Barre City and Galfetti of Barre
4 Town
5 Referred to Committee on
6 Date:
7 Subject: Internal security and public safety; emergency management;
8 miscellaneous amendments relating to flood response and resilience
9 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to require the
10 Agency of Transportation to provide funding to rural and flood-prone
11 municipalities for upgrading infrastructure, including roads, bridges, culverts,
12 gravel roads, and emergency access routes. This bill also proposes to require
13 the Agency to design and implement stormwater management system
14 upgrades, including larger culverts, reinforced ditches, and drainage systems
15 capable of managing severe weather events. This bill also proposes to require
16 the Agency to develop a statewide asset management system to track flood
17 response equipment availability, trained personnel, and critical resource
18 locations. This bill also proposes to procure and provide State-funded regional
19 fleets of vacuum trucks and other heavy-duty equipment to assist
20 municipalities with debris removal and maintenance of flood mitigation
21 systems. This bill also proposes to enhance transparency and communication
