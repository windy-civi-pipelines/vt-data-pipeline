6 Open Meeting Law
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to amend
8 Vermont’s Open Meeting Law to state that annual municipal meetings and
9 representative town meetings are not “public bodies” that are subject to the
10 Open Meeting Law. This bill also proposes to provide that a public body may
11 move to enter executive session to discuss the interest rates of publicly
12 financed loans.
13 An act relating to amendments to Vermont’s Open Meeting Law

It is the intent of the General Assembly that section 5 of this act amend 13
V.S.A. § 1026 to conform subdivision (a)(4) of that section with the
constitutional requirements articulated in the Supreme Court of Vermont
decision State v. Colby, 185 Vt. 464 (2009).
