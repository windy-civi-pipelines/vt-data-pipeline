14 The purpose of this act is to expressly include the option of an affirmation

16 throughout Titles 1–10 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated (V.S.A.), except in

14 The purpose of this act is to expressly include the option of an affirmation
15 in each instance in which an oath or swearing is authorized or required
16 throughout Titles 1–10 of the Vermont Statutes Annotated (V.S.A.), except in
17 interstate compacts or as otherwise limited by law. The act also makes other
18 minor, technical amendments to the language of the affected statutes for
19 readability and consistency with V.S.A. style. It is the intent of the General
20 Assembly that the technical amendments in this act shall not supersede
21 substantive changes contained in other bills enacted by the General Assembly
