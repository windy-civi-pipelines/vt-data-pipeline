8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to remove
9 grievances from the Labor Relations Board workload and to require that the
10 final step in the grievance process be binding arbitration. This bill also
11 proposes to set deadlines for the handling of unfair labor practices before the
12 Labor Relations Board.
