2025 Page 1 of 1
1 H.154
3 Veteran
4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT
6 It is the intent of the General Assembly to honor the special value of the
7 military service that the veterans of the U.S. Armed Forces have contributed to
8 the security and well-being of our nation by designating November as the
9 Vermont Month of the Veteran.
10 Sec. 2. 1 V.S.A. § 378 is added to read:
11 § 378. VERMONT MONTH OF THE VETERAN
12 November of each year is designated as the Vermont Month of the Veteran.
13 Sec. 3. EFFECTIVE DATE
14 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
VT LEG #381146 v.1
