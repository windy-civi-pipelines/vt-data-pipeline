3 § 2449g. AGE ASSURANCE PRIVACY
4 (a) Privacy protections for age assurance data. During the process of
5 conducting age assurance, covered businesses and processors shall:
6 (1) only collect personal data of a user that is strictly necessary for age
7 assurance;
8 (2) immediately upon determining whether a user is a covered minor,
9 delete any personal data collected of that user for age assurance, except the
10 determination of the user’s age range;
11 (3) not use any personal data of a user collected for age assurance for
12 any other purpose;
13 (4) not combine personal data of a user collected for age assurance,
14 except the determination of the user’s age range, with any other personal data
15 of the user;
16 (5) not disclose personal data of a user collected for age assurance to a
17 third party that is not a processor; and
18 (6) implement a review process to allow users to appeal their age
19 determination.
