1 H.199
2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representatives Burrows of West Windsor, Bartholomew of
4 Hartland, Coffin of Cavendish, Cordes of Bristol, and McGill of
5 Bridport
6 Referred to Committee on
7 Date:
8 Subject: Education; maintenance of public schools; tuition; public schools;
9 excess spending
10 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to limit the
11 tuition rate charged by a receiving public school to the average announced
12 tuition of union elementary schools or union high schools, as applicable. It
13 would also prohibit a receiving public school from charging sending school
14 districts any additional fees for special education, availability reservation, or
15 other fees that cannot be accounted for. This bill also proposes to eliminate
16 tuition paid by a nonoperating school district from the district’s education
17 spending for the purposes of calculating excess spending pursuant to 32 V.S.A.
18 § 5401(12).
19 An act relating to tuition charged by public schools
