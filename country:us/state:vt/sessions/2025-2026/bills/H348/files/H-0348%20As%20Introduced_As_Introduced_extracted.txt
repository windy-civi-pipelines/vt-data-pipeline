6 Subject: Labor; safety; Extreme Temperature Worker Protection Act
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create an
8 Extreme Temperature Worker Protection Act to protect workers exposed to
9 extreme heat or cold in the workplace from occupational illnesses, injuries, and
10 death by requiring employers to create a plan to evaluate and control
11 temperature-related hazards in the workplace.
12 An act relating to protecting workers from extreme temperatures in the
13 workplace
