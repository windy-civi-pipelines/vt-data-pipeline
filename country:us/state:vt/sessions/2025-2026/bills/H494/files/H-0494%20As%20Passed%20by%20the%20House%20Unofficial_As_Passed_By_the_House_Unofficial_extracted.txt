5 Sec. 1. LEGISLATIVE INTENT
6 (a) It is the intent of the General Assembly that of the $111,965,288.44
7 authorized in this act, not more than $61,614,761.44 shall be appropriated in
8 the first year of the biennium, and the remainder shall be appropriated in the
9 second year.
10 (b) It is the intent of the General Assembly that in the second year of the
11 biennium, any amendments to the appropriations or authorities granted in this
12 act shall take the form of the Capital Construction and State Bonding
13 Adjustment Bill. It is the intent of the General Assembly that unless otherwise
14 indicated, all appropriations in this act are subject to capital budget adjustment.
