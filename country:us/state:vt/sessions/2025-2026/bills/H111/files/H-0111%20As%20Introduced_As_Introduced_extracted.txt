1 H.111
2 Introduced by Representatives Toof of St. Albans Town and Hango of
3 Berkshire
4 Referred to Committee on
5 Date:
6 Subject: Motor vehicles; registration fees; volunteer firefighters
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to waive the
8 registration fee for a volunteer firefighter’s personal motor vehicle when the
9 volunteer firefighter has the written authorization to use the motor vehicle for
10 emergency fire or rescue activities.
11 An act relating to waived registration fees for volunteer firefighter motor
12 vehicles
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. 23 V.S.A. § 376(c) is amended to read:
15 (c)(1) The registration fee for registration of a motor truck, trailer,
16 ambulance, or other motor vehicle, owned by a volunteer fire department or
17 other volunteer firefighting organization or other organization conducting
18 rescue operations and used solely for fire fighting firefighting or rescue
19 purposes shall be $15.00 in lieu of fees otherwise specified in this chapter. A
VT LEG #380054 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.111
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 motor vehicle or trailer registered under this section shall be plainly marked on
2 both sides of the body or cab to indicate its ownership.
3 (2) The registration fee for registration of a motor vehicle owned or
4 leased by a volunteer firefighter and issued a permit for sirens and red or red
5 and white signal lamps pursuant to subdivision 1252(b)(2)(A) of this title shall
6 be $0.00 in lieu of fees otherwise specified in this chapter.
7 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
8 This act shall take effect on July 1, 2025.
