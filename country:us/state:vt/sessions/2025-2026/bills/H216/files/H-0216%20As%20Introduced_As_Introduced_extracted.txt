6 Subject: Public service; energy; renewable energy; Affordable Heat Act; Clean

8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to authorize the
9 Clean Heat Standard rules to be adopted with a price cap on the cost of clean
10 heat credits. The price cap shall ensure that the cost of the Clean Heat
11 Standard Program will not raise the delivered cost of fossil heating fuels more
12 than $0.10 per gallon for the first five years.
