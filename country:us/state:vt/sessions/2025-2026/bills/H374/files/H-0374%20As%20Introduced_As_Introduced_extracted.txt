1 H.374
2 Introduced by Representative Krasnow of South Burlington
3 Referred to Committee on
4 Date:
5 Subject: Labor; employment practices; minimum wage
6 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to repeal the
7 Commissioner of Labor’s authority to recommend a subminimum wage for
8 individuals with a disability.
9 An act relating to repealing the Commissioner of Labor’s authority to
10 recommend a subminimum wage for individuals with a disability
11 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
12 Sec. 1. 21 V.S.A. § 385 is amended to read:
13 § 385. ADMINISTRATION
14 The Commissioner and the Commissioner’s authorized representatives have
15 full power and authority for all the following:
16 * * *
17 (5) To recommend a suitable scale of rates for learners, and apprentices,
18 and persons with disabilities, which may be less than the regular minimum
19 wage rate for experienced workers without disabilities.
VT LEG #380216 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.374
2025 Page 2 of 2
1 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE
2 This act shall take effect on passage.
VT LEG #380216 v.1
