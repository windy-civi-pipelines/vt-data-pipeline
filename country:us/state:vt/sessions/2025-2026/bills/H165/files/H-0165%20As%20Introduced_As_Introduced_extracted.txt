1 H.165
2 SHORT FORM
3 Introduced by Representative Headrick of Burlington
4 Referred to Committee on
5 Date:
6 Subject: Education
7 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to create an
8 Inclusive Schools Protection Fund to offset reductions in federal funding for
9 inclusive programs, prioritizing LGBTQ+ student support programs,
10 antidiscrimination initiatives, inclusive curriculum development, and training
11 for educators on equity and inclusivity; to provide direct funding to school
12 districts for inclusive programs; to protect funding for gender and sexuality
13 alliances, equity coordinators in schools, and mental health and antibullying
14 programs targeting marginalized students; to task the Agency of Education
15 with reporting annually on the efficacy of the State’s funding efforts to support
16 inclusivity; and to develop alternative revenue streams to supplement the
17 Inclusive Schools Protection Fund.
18 An act relating to funding inclusive schools
19 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
