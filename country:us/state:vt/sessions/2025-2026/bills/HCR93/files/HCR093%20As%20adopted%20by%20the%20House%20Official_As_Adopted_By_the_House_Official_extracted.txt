State of Vermont
House of Representatives
Montpelier, Vermont
Concurrent House Resolution
H.C.R. 93
House concurrent resolution congratulating Kathleen Lynch of Burlington on being named as the
2025 Vermont Mother of the Year
Whereas, Kathleen Lynch was born to Fred and Kathleen Connor in Worcester, Massachusetts, and
faith and family were central in the Connor family, and
Whereas, after graduating from the Saint Vincent Hospital School of Nursing, she devoted an
astounding 47 years to surgical nursing, primarily at the hospital now known as the University of
Vermont Medical Center (UVMC), and
Whereas, she married now-retired UVMC anesthesiologist Dr. Terrance Lynch, and they are the
proud parents of seven children and have two grandchildren, and
Whereas, in 2010, her family assisted in the early Champlain Valley Down Syndrome Group’s
activities, and the Lynch family continues to support the Buddy Walk in Burlington, and
Whereas, since 1935, American Mothers, Inc. has annually, with the participation of local members,
designated a Mother of the Year for every state, the District of Columbia, and Puerto Rico, and from the
2025 selections, a National Mother of the Year will be chosen at a convention to be held this year in
Puerto Rico, and
Whereas, a Mother of the Year has demonstrated a strong commitment to the role of motherhood and
serving her community, and
Whereas, Kathleen Lynch has been deservedly honored as the 2025 Vermont Mother of the Year, now
therefore be it
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives:
That the General Assembly congratulates Kathleen Lynch of Burlington on being named as the 2025
Vermont Mother of the Year, and be it further
Resolved: That the Secretary of State be directed to send a copy of this resolution to Kathleen
Lynch.
