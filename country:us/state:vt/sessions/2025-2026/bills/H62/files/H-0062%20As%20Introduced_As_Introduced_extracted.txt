BILL AS INTRODUCED H.62
2025 Page 2 of 4
1 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
2 Sec. 1. REPEAL
3 10 V.S.A. chapter 24 (Vermont Climate Council and Climate Action Plan)
4 is repealed.
5 Sec. 2. 10 V.S.A. § 578 is amended to read:
6 § 578. GREENHOUSE GAS REDUCTION REQUIREMENTS GOALS
7 (a) Greenhouse gas reduction requirements goals. It is the goal of Vermont
8 shall to reduce emissions of greenhouse gases from within the geographical
9 boundaries of the State and those emissions outside the boundaries of the State
10 that are caused by the use of energy in Vermont, as measured and inventoried
11 pursuant to section 582 of this title, by:
12 * * *
13 (c) Implementation of State programs to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
14 In order to facilitate the State’s compliance with the goals established in this
15 section, all State agencies shall consider, whenever practicable, any increase or
16 decrease in greenhouse gas emissions in their decision-making procedures with
17 respect to the purchase and use of equipment and goods; the siting,
18 construction, and maintenance of buildings; the assignment of personnel; and
19 the planning, design, and operation of programs, services, and infrastructure.
20 * * *
VT LEG #379037 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.62
2025 Page 3 of 4
1 Sec. 3. 30 V.S.A. § 202a is amended to read:
2 § 202a. STATE ENERGY POLICY
3 It is the general policy of the State of Vermont:
4 * * *
5 (3) To meet Vermont’s energy service needs in a manner that will
6 achieve the greenhouse gas emissions reductions requirements goals pursuant
7 to 10 V.S.A § 578 and is consistent with the Vermont Climate Action Plan
8 adopted and updated pursuant to 10 V.S.A. § 592.
9 Sec. 4. 30 V.S.A. § 202b is amended to read:
10 § 202b. STATE COMPREHENSIVE ENERGY PLAN
11 (a) The Department of Public Service, in conjunction with other State
12 agencies designated by the Governor, shall prepare a State Comprehensive
13 Energy Plan covering at least a 20-year period. The Plan shall seek to
14 implement the State energy policy set forth in section 202a of this title,
15 including meeting the State’s greenhouse gas emissions reductions
16 requirements goals pursuant to 10 V.S.A. § 578, and shall be consistent with
17 the relevant goals of 24 V.S.A. § 4302 and with the Vermont Climate Action
18 Plan adopted and updated pursuant to 10 V.S.A. § 592. The State
19 Comprehensive Energy Plan shall include:
20 * * *
