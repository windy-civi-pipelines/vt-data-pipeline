4 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
5 Sec. 1. CHARTER AMENDMENT APPROVAL
6 The General Assembly approves the amendment of the charter of the Town
7 of Barre as set forth in this act. Voters approved the proposals of amendment
8 on May 14, 2024.
9 Sec. 2. 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 101 is amended to read:
10 CHAPTER 101. TOWN OF BARRE
11 * * *
12 § 10. ELECTED OFFICERS; GENERAL
13 (a) The following officers shall be elected by the legal voters of the Town
14 of Barre and voting for the officers shall be by Australian ballot:
15 * * *
16 (2) three auditors; [Repealed.]
17 (3) one Clerk; and
18 (4) one Moderator.
19 * * *
20 (f) The Office of Auditor may be abolished and replaced by an annual
21 financial audit of all funds of the Town by a public accountant, licensed in
22 Vermont, provided the voters authorize the actions at any annual or special

1 (d) All fees prescribed by State law, charter, ordinance, or otherwise shall
2 be collected for the benefit of the Town and paid to the Town Treasurer. No
3 fees shall be used directly by any receiving officer or enure inure directly to
4 the officer.

6 Sec. 3. REPEAL
7 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 101, § 12 (Town of Barre; Auditors) is repealed.
