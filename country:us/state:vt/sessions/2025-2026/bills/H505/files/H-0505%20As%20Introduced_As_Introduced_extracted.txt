1 H.505
2 Introduced by Representatives Galfetti of Barre Town and McFaun of Barre
3 Town
4 Referred to Committee on
5 Date:
6 Subject: Municipal and county government; municipal charters; Town of
7 Barre; amendments
8 Statement of purpose of bill as introduced: This bill proposes to approve
9 amendments to the charter of the Town of Barre to repeal the Town’s Office of
10 Auditor.
11 An act relating to approval of amendments to the charter of the Town of
12 Barre
13 It is hereby enacted by the General Assembly of the State of Vermont:
14 Sec. 1. CHARTER AMENDMENT APPROVAL
15 The General Assembly approves the amendment of the charter of the Town
16 of Barre as set forth in this act. Voters approved the proposals of amendment
17 on May 14, 2024.
18 Sec. 2. 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 101 is amended to read:
19 CHAPTER 101. TOWN OF BARRE
20 * * *
VT LEG #382529 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.505
2025 Page 2 of 3
1 § 10. ELECTED OFFICERS; GENERAL
2 (a) The following officers shall be elected by the legal voters of the Town
3 of Barre and voting for the officers shall be by Australian ballot:
4 * * *
5 (2) three auditors; [Repealed.]
6 (3) one Clerk; and
7 (4) one Moderator.
8 * * *
9 (f) The Office of Auditor may be abolished and replaced by an annual
10 financial audit of all funds of the Town by a public accountant, licensed in
11 Vermont, provided the voters authorize the actions at any annual or special
12 Town meeting. The vote to eliminate the Office of Auditor shall remain in
13 effect until rescinded by majority vote of the legal voters present and voting at
14 an annual or special meeting duly warned for that purpose. Unless otherwise
15 provided by law, the Selectboard shall provide for all other auditor duties to be
16 performed. The term of office of any auditor in the office on the date the Town
17 votes to eliminate that office shall expire on the 45th day after the vote or on
18 the date that the Selectboard enters into a contract with a public accountant
19 under this section, whichever occurs first. [Repealed.]
20 * * *
VT LEG #382529 v.1
BILL AS INTRODUCED H.505
2025 Page 3 of 3
1 § 39. COMPENSATION AND FEES
2 (a) The Town election shall annually vote the compensation to be paid to
3 the following officers:
4 * * *
5 (2) Auditors; [Repealed.]
6 * * *
7 (b) The Selectboard shall annually consider and from time to time shall set
8 the compensation of the following officers:
9 * * *
10 (7) Board of Abatement; and
11 * * *
12 (d) All fees prescribed by State law, charter, ordinance, or otherwise shall
13 be collected for the benefit of the Town and paid to the Town Treasurer. No
14 fees shall be used directly by any receiving officer or enure inure directly to
15 the officer.
16 * * *
17 Sec. 3. REPEAL
18 24 App. V.S.A. chapter 101, § 12 (Town of Barre; Auditors) is repealed.
19 Sec. 4. EFFECTIVE DATE
20 This act shall take effect on passage.
